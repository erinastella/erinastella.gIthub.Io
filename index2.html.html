About Erina

A voice
for the nations

Erina Stella Nakiberu is a Kampala-based gospel musician whose sound transcends borders. With a voice that carries both power and tenderness, she has become a beloved figure in Uganda's worship music scene.

Her music blends contemporary gospel with traditional Ugandan rhythms, creating an atmosphere of reverence and joy. She has ministered at major conferences, led worship across East Africa, and released multiple singles that resonate with audiences worldwide.
