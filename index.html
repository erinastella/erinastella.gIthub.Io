A Voice for the Kingdom

Growing up in the vibrant heart of Kampala, my musical journey began in the church choir. I discovered early on that music isn't just about perfect pitch—it's a vessel for spiritual transformation.

For over a decade, I've dedicated my voice to gospel ministry, blending contemporary worship styles with rich, rhythmic Ugandan influences. My mission is simple: to create an atmosphere where hearts encounter divine presence.
