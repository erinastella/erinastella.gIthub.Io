Bio

Erina Stella Nakiberu is a gospel musician, teacher, and mother from Gganda, Nansana, Wakiso. A devoted Seventh‑day Adventist, she uses music as a ministry tool, blending rich Ugandan rhythms with contemporary worship.

She has released several singles and is known for her powerful voice and heartfelt lyrics that inspire faith and hope. Erina is available for worship leading, conferences, and vocal features.
