About Erina

Erina Stella Nakiberu is a gospel musician from Bukereere, Central Uganda. Her music centers on worship, testimony, and community healing. She leads worship at regional gatherings and composes songs that blend modern arrangements with East African melodic sensibilities.

Performance highlights include community outreach concerts, church residencies, and collaborative recordings with regional choirs. Erina mentors young vocalists and runs workshops focused on worship leadership.
