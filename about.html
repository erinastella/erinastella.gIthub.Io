About Erina Stella

Teacher • Gospel Musician • Mother

Living my calling in Gganda, Nansana, Wakiso. A Seventh‑Day Adventist rooted in faith, music, and community.

Listen to My Music Get in Touch

My Story

I discovered my love for music as a little girl singing in the children’s choir at church. What began as a simple joy soon became a lifelong ministry. Today, as a gospel musician, I have the privilege of writing songs that point hearts toward hope, grace, and the unfailing love of God.

Alongside music, I’ve always been drawn to the classroom. I serve as a teacher, helping young minds grow not only academically but spiritually. Whether I’m leading worship or standing before a blackboard, my goal is the same: to inspire and equip the next generation.

Family is the heartbeat of everything I do. I am a proud mother, and the joys and challenges of parenting continually shape my music and my message. My home is in Gganda, Nansana, Wakiso – a vibrant community that keeps me grounded and grateful.

I am a committed Seventh‑Day Adventist Christian. My faith is not just a label; it’s the lens through which I see the world, create music, and interact with everyone I meet. The Sabbath rest, the hope of the soon return of Jesus, and the call to serve others are the pillars of my life.

Where I Call Home

Gganda, Nansana, Wakiso District – just a stone’s throw from the bustling heart of Kampala. This is where I live, teach, worship, and raise my family. The warmth of this community echoes in every note I sing.
